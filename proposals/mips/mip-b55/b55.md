# MIP-B55: Moonwell Vault Incentive Management

### Summary

This proposal seeks to renew and adjust incentive allocations for select
Moonwell vaults for the upcoming incentive period. The goal is to maintain
competitive yields on core assets, support sustained liquidity, and ensure that
incentive spending remains aligned with vault utilization and strategic
importance.

The proposed incentive window runs from **January 16, 2026 through February 2,
2026**.

---

### Background

Moonwell uses vault-level incentives to attract and retain liquidity in
strategically important assets, support borrowing activity, and strengthen
overall protocol health. These incentives are intentionally distributed in
discrete, time-bounded windows to allow governance to regularly evaluate
effectiveness and make adjustments based on market conditions.

The current incentive period is concluding, necessitating a renewal decision to
avoid disruption to liquidity and user participation across key vaults.

### Proposal

This proposal authorizes the distribution of WELL incentives across the
following vaults for the upcoming incentive period:

| Vault     | Incentive Amount (WELL) |
| --------- | ----------------------- |
| USDC      | 1,356,324.84            |
| WETH      | 454,278.62              |
| EURC      | 271,049.85              |
| cbBTC     | 110,946.01              |
| **Total** | **2,192,599.32 WELL**   |

The incentive period will begin on **January 16, 2026** and conclude on
**February 2, 2026**.

### Rationale

USDC receives the largest share of incentives due to its role as the primary
liquidity and borrowing asset on Moonwell, making it critical to overall
protocol usage and revenue generation. WETH incentives support core DeFi
activity and sustained borrowing demand. EURC incentives encourage
diversification of stablecoin liquidity and support non-USD use cases. cbBTC
incentives maintain exposure to Bitcoin-denominated liquidity while supporting
strategically relevant assets.

### Voting Options

- **For:** Approve the proposed vault incentive allocations and incentive
  period.
- **Against:** Reject the proposed incentive allocations.
- **Abstain:** Express no preference.
