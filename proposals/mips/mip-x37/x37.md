# MIP-X37: Reserve Reduction for Token Swap

## Summary

This proposal reduces reserves from three Base markets and transfers them to an
EOA for swapping to VIRTUALS and cbXRP.

## Actions

- Reduce 347 WETH from MOONWELL_WETH market
- Reduce 490,000 USDC from MOONWELL_USDC market
- Reduce 3 cbBTC from MOONWELL_cbBTC market
- Transfer all tokens to ANA_EOA (0x9917Ea34179D87F06C8b9D4AfB8BD78248B434ef)
