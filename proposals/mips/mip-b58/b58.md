# MIP-B58: MFAM Onboarding and cbETH Incident Restitution

## Summary

This proposal has two primary objectives:

1. **Provide restitution to wallets impacted by the cbETH incident** through a
   combination of recovered funds and DAO treasury support.
2. **Onboard MFAM and stkMFAM holders into the Moonwell ecosystem** through a
   WELL staking allocation and a targeted stkWELL airdrop. More details on the
   stkWELL calculation may be found in this airdrop calculation app found
   [here](https://mfam-drop.moonwell.workers.dev/)

**Outside of this proposal, further restitution can come from the DAO through a
vote on future protocol revenue.**

If passed, the proposal will:

- Transfer 310,301.6 USDC from the Moonwell DAO treasury to a designated
  multisig.
- The USDC will be swapped to ETH, which will then be combined with 19.90098 ETH
  recovered by BitcoinVN from the attacker to partially repay affected wallets
  proportionally.
- Transfer 19,095,528 WELL from the Foundation treasury to be staked and
  distributed as stkWELL to MFAM and stkMFAM holders, based on the snapshot
  taken at the time of the cbETH Recovery Plan and MFAM Onboarding forum post.

This proposal simultaneously addresses user restitution while expanding
Moonwell's long-term aligned community through stkWELL participation.

## Background

A recent incident involving cbETH resulted in losses to several users. Since the
event, efforts have been made to recover funds and determine the most fair and
practical method of compensating affected wallets.

Through external recovery efforts, BitcoinVN successfully recovered 19.90098 ETH
from the attacker. While meaningful, this recovered amount alone is insufficient
to fully reimburse impacted users. As a result, additional support from the DAO
treasury is proposed to improve the recovery outcome.

Separately, the Moonwell ecosystem has the opportunity to expand its aligned
user base by onboarding holders from the MFAM ecosystem. These users are already
familiar with DeFi primitives and represent a strong potential cohort of
long-term participants in Moonwell governance and staking.

Allocating WELL to be staked and distributed as stkWELL enables these users to
become aligned with the protocol while also strengthening Moonwell's staking
participation.

## Proposal

If this proposal passes, the following actions will occur:

### 1. cbETH Incident Restitution

- **310,301.6 USDC** will be transferred from the **Moonwell DAO treasury** to a
  designated **multisig wallet**.
- The multisig will **swap the USDC for ETH**.
- The swapped ETH will be **combined with the 19.90098 ETH recovered by
  BitcoinVN**.
- The resulting ETH will be **distributed proportionally to wallets impacted by
  the cbETH incident**.

Distribution calculations will be based on verified loss data from the incident.

### 2. MFAM Ecosystem Onboarding

- **19,095,528 WELL** will be transferred from the **Moonwell Foundation
  treasury**.
- These tokens will be **staked to mint stkWELL**.
- The stkWELL will be **airdropped to MFAM and stkMFAM holders**.

Eligibility will be determined using the **snapshot taken at the time of
Fechucky's forum post**.

## Rationale

This proposal is designed to accomplish two important goals simultaneously.

### Restoring User Trust

Providing restitution to affected cbETH users demonstrates Moonwell's commitment
to responsible ecosystem stewardship. While the DAO was not solely responsible
for the incident, assisting impacted users helps preserve trust and reinforces
Moonwell's reputation as a user-aligned protocol.

### Expanding the Moonwell Ecosystem

The Apollo community represents a cohort of active DeFi users who can become
valuable long-term participants in Moonwell governance and staking.

By distributing **stkWELL rather than liquid WELL**, the proposal encourages:

- Long-term alignment
- Governance participation

## Voting Options

**Option 1 — For**: Approve the proposal to:

- Transfer **310,301.6 USDC** from the DAO treasury for cbETH restitution
- Swap the USDC to ETH and combine with **19.90098 recovered ETH**
- Repay impacted wallets proportionally
- Transfer **19,095,528 WELL** from the Foundation treasury
- Stake the WELL and distribute **stkWELL to MFAM and stkMFAM holders**

**Option 2 — Against**: Reject the proposal. No treasury transfers, repayments,
or stkWELL distributions will occur.

**Option 3 — Abstain**: Indicate neutrality on the proposal.
