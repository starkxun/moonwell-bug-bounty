## MIP-B56: Transfer Risk Curatorship for Moonwell Vaults (Base) to Anthias Labs

### **Summary**

This proposal seeks approval to transfer the risk curator role for Moonwell's
Morpho vaults on Base from BlockAnalitica / B.Protocol to Anthias Labs. These
changes will affect the following Base vaults: USDC, WETH, EURC, cbBTC, mwUSDC,
and meUSDC.

This change will unify risk management under a single team already responsible
for Moonwell protocol risk, reduce redundant costs, and improve operational
efficiency which will result in cost savings and improved net revenue.

### **Background**

Moonwell currently engages multiple external risk providers across different
components of the protocol, including Morpho vaults. While this structure has
supported protocol safety, it introduces:

- Overlapping responsibilities across risk providers
- Higher recurring costs
- Increased coordination and governance overhead

Anthias Labs already serves as Moonwell’s primary risk management partner at the
protocol level. However, risk curation for Morpho vaults on Base is currently
handled separately by BlockAnalitica / B.Protocol, resulting in fragmented risk
ownership.

### **Proposal**

If approved, this proposal will:

- Transfer the risk curator role for Moonwell vaults deployed on Base
  - From: BlockAnalitica / B.Protocol
  - To: Anthias Labs
- Scope the change exclusively to Base vaults
- Preserve all existing vault configurations unless modified through future
  governance proposals
