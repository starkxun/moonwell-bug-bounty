# MIP-B53: Moonwell WETH Market Management

### **Summary**

This proposal deploys a new ownership wrapper for the WETH market on Base,
allowing the protocol to receive and manage funds during reserve operations. The
wrapper enables Moonwell to reduce WETH reserves without additional execution
hiccups while maintaining governance control.

### **Background**

The WETH market on Base relies on native ETH when reserves are adjusted, but the
administrative contract cannot receive ETH directly. Because of this issue,
reserve reduction operations cannot be executed as easily.

To address this problem, Moonwell contributors have designed an ownership
wrapper that can receive native ETH, convert it to WETH, and forward all
administrative actions. This creates a clean workflow for reserve management.

To be clear, this only effects the WETH market on Base.

### **Proposal**

This proposal introduces the MWETHOwnerWrapper, audited by Halborn, a contract
that serves as the new admin for the WETH market while remaining governed by the
existing Governor module.

Deployment steps included in this proposal:

1. Deploy the MWethOwnerWrapper implementation
2. Deploy its TransparentUpgradeableProxy
3. Initialize the wrapper with the WETH market, the WETH token, and
   TEMPORAL_GOVERNOR as owner
4. Set the wrapper as pending admin of the WETH market
5. Accept admin through the wrapper to finalize migration
6. Reduce the current WETH reserves (347 WETH) to cover some of the remaining
   bad debt.

### **Rationale**

Deploying the MWethOwnerWrapper provides a reliable way for Moonwell to manage
WETH reserves on Base. By enabling the wrapper to receive native ETH and
automatically convert it to WETH, reserve operations can execute consistently
without execution-context limitations.

Additionally, Moonwell's WETH reserves currently have around 347 WETH, or about
1M in USD terms. This allows the protocol to pay for another 20% of the bad debt
incurred from 10/10 and the November oracle issue, putting Moonwell at halfway
through paying the bad debt back.

### **Voting Options**

- **For:** Approve deployment of the MWethOwnerWrapper and migrate WETH market
  admin.
- **Against:** Do not deploy the wrapper; reserve management remains limited.
- **Abstain:** No preference.
