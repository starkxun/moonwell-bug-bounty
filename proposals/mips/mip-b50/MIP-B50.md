# MIP-B50: Moonwell Morpho Vault and stkWELL Incentive Campaigns

**Author(s):** Moonwell DAO **Submission Date:** October 14th, 2025

## Proposal Summary

This proposal creates Merkle incentive campaigns for Moonwell's MetaMorpho
vaults (USDC, WETH, EURC, and cbBTC) and introduces a new incentive campaign for
stkWELL holders to encourage participation in the safety module.

## Campaign Distribution

The proposal distributes WELL tokens across multiple campaigns:

- **USDC MetaMorpho Vault Campaign**
- **WETH MetaMorpho Vault Campaign**
- **EURC MetaMorpho Vault Campaign**
- **cbBTC MetaMorpho Vault Campaign**
- **stkWELL Safety Module Campaign** (NEW)

## Implementation

If this proposal passes, the following onchain actions will be executed:

1. Bridge WELL tokens from Moonbeam to Base
2. Approve Merkle campaign creator to spend WELL tokens
3. Accept Merkle campaign creator conditions
4. Create campaigns for all MetaMorpho vaults
5. Create stkWELL safety module campaign

## Voting Options

- **For:** Create the proposed incentive campaigns for MetaMorpho vaults and
  stkWELL
- **Against:** Do not create the incentive campaigns
- **Abstain**

## Conclusion

These incentive campaigns will drive liquidity to Moonwell's MetaMorpho vaults
and encourage participation in the safety module, strengthening the protocol's
security and growth.
